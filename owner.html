Owner (Web&App)



The biggest priority for most successful business owners is customer satisfaction and repeat business. With MealMe you have perfect accessibility to the key control points within an application to ensure exactly those objectives. MealMe gives you live visibility of all order request for both delivery and reservations. We also show you the progress, and time projections of these orders with the option to accept or deny any request as they come in. You are also able to turn your availability function off entirely at your convenience should you feel the restaurant needs time to focus on the current parties and deliveries? With MealMe the owner application also controls the menu in several ways. Did you run out of the special? In one touch of a button you can disable any menu item for any specified amount of time. Is the traffic just not coming in today? MealMe gives you the option at your fingertips to run last minute specials that get seen directly by our consumers all over the city. Would you like to delegate some of these controls to your trusted manager or admin? MealMe lets you customize your business settings at any time.
