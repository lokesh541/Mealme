User (App)



Using MealMe you are able to order your meal through our revolutionary application prior to arriving to the restaurant of your choosing. You simply go through your personalized profile to select your desired meal of choice. Find what you want? Once you have customized your meal we will automatically calculate the preparation time and your estimated time of arrival to the restaurant via GPS to ensure you just need to arrive and enjoy. Want MealMe to come to you? We would love to. Customize your order to your satisfaction and we will come at your convenience. We also save all of your food preferences, allergies, and how to prepare your items to your liking which of course you can update at any time. Don’t worry we’ll keep you updated along the way via GPS so you know exactly when to expect your meal.
