Brief about our Management:


Rama Krishna Gande is the Chief Executive Officer of KJ INFO LLC, a business consulting and global information technology services company headquartered in Hoboken, NJ. He is an Accomplished Entrepreneur and a Transformational Leader having been part of the outsourcing market for over 16 years, he remains involved in all aspects of the Business including Product Development, Staffing, Recruitment and Sales. Under his Leadership, the company has grown to achieve annual revenue of over $2 Million. He holds a Duel Masters’ Degree in Business Administration with a major in Human Resources, Information Technology and Marketing.

Chaitanya Parvathaneni is a Dynamic Business Development Manager of KJ Info LLC from a Marketing and Sales Background. He has over 18 years of invaluable experience in Managerial Capacity working for Multinational Companies. He holds a Duel Masters’ Degree in Business Administration with a major in Marketing, International Business and Hospitality. He is Responsible for all New Client Acquisitions, Managing the Existing Clients and interact with Clients daily. He is Accountable for Revenue Generation as well as Client Relationship Management. As a Head of Business Development Team, he would manage the entire Sales Department for acquiring new clients as well as Marketing our Product Prototype in US.

Rageeth Sivasankaran is a Tech Savvy Architect with over 13 years of experience in the IT industry. He has a Bachelors’ Degree in Computer Science Engineering and have worked with top Retailers like Target, 1800 Flowers and Home Depot in US and Argos, Sainsburys in the UK. With a knack in executing complex IT projects, he will lead the entire technical and product team from Inception to Production. By involving with the team daily he will monitor and track the progress and needs of the technical competencies required to execute the project. With an extensive technical expertise, he will be able to choose the right technical stack, infrastructure requirement, third party integration which meets today’s dynamic and changing customer needs .
